Bring the preset buttons of your Bose SoundTouch back to life! SoundTouch Bridge lets your speakers stream internet radio again and recall your favourite stations straight from the physical preset buttons, just like before. Homey runs on your local network as an always-on bridge between the speaker and the web radio you choose.

After Bose shut down the SoundTouch cloud service, the preset buttons stopped recalling stations and internet radio streaming no longer worked, leaving only AirPlay, Bluetooth and AUX. This app restores that core experience: pair your speaker, assign a direct internet radio stream to each of the six preset slots, and the buttons work again. Preset names are synced to the speaker display, and you can also start any preset or stream from Homey and from Flows. It is not a full replacement for the original cloud features, but it brings the day-to-day experience back to a good level. Only direct HTTP audio streams are supported; HTTPS, HLS and playlist links are not.
